Segway'n USA

Document about my trip around the US on my very own Segway!

August 20, 2005

Well i made it 1200 miles already, and my pass, through some interesting places on the way: Walla Walla, WA, Magic City, ID, Bountiful, UT, Last Chance, CO, Whhy, AZ, and Truth or Consequences, NM.

July 14, 2005

I saw some Burma Shave style signs on the side of the road today: "Passing cars, When you can't see, May get you, A glimpse, Of eternity." I definetely won't be passing any car.

June 2, 2005

My first day of the trip! I can’t believe I finally got everything packed and ready to go. Because I’m on a Segway, I wasn’t able to bring a whole lot with me: cellphone, iPod, digital camera, and a protein bar. Just the essentials. As Lao Tzu would have said, “A journey of a thousand miles begins with one Segway.”
